About Our Company

About Us

What started as a dream to redefine what it means to wear something authentic, turned into a boutique fashion house that carries both timeless classics and bold trends. Every piece we design goes through thoughtful craftsmanship and inspired detail so that when you wear it, you feel empowered, stylish, and uniquely you.

Welcome to Leyzeb Closet Fashion House where creativity, quality, and confidence come together.

Based in Nairobi at Woodley Shopping Centre, we are more than just a clothing store. We are a vision brought to life: handcrafted designs, precision cut-and-sewn pieces, and fashion that feels made for you.
